Sustainable Living: Small Changes, Big Impact

Sustainability doesn’t require massive lifestyle shifts—small consistent habits can make a huge difference.

1. Reduce Plastic

Carry reusable bags, bottles, and cutlery to cut down on plastic waste.

2. Save Energy

Switch to LED bulbs and unplug unused devices to lower carbon footprint.

3. Eat Local

Buying local and seasonal produce supports farmers and reduces emissions.

4. Recycle & Reuse

Before throwing things away, find ways to reuse or recycle them.
